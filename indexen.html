Viet Nam Children's Climate Risk Index (VNCCRI)

The Viet Nam Children's Climate Risk Index (VNCCRI) aims to assess the level of risk from the impacts of natural disasters and climate change on children based on their access to essential services such as education, health care, clean water, and sanitation, school…

The index uses data and information available at the global, national, and local levels to calculate and assess the impact of natural disasters and climate change, and the children's vulnerability to these events. This indicator is presented as a map to the commune level to visually assess and compare the level of risk in the localities.

The VNCCRI index and mapping tool is intended to assist governments, local authorities, stakeholders, the private sector, and communities in risk information-based planning, investment and policy change.
